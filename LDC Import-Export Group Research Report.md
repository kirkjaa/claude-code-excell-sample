## 2. Company Overview: LDC Import-Export Group

LDC Import-Export Group, established in 2011, is a prominent business entity in Laos PDR. The company initially focused on providing services and distributing imported and domestic products in Vientiane Capital under the U-Express brand, inspired by the French retail company Système U [1]. Since 2014, LDC Import-Export has expanded its retail presence with Jiffy convenience stores, particularly at PTT gas stations and within local communities, gaining significant consumer trust [1].

**Vision and Mission:**
LDC Group's vision is to become a leading company in Laos, operating a modern and comprehensive retail business with excellent standards and service. Their core mission is built on four pillars [1]:

1.  **Service:** Providing standardized and prompt service to build customer confidence.
2.  **Products:** Offering a wide variety of high-quality, cost-effective products that meet customer needs.
3.  **Partnerships:** Fostering unity and maintaining good relationships within the organization and with business partners.
4.  **Standards:** Building trust with customers and employees while being attentive to society and the environment.

**Operations and Workforce:**
The company has established a systematic management structure covering all its subsidiaries, focusing on sustainable growth. LDC Group employs over 400 staff members [1]. In the retail and distribution sector, it operates 33 branches across various provinces, including Vientiane Capital, Luang Prabang, Khamwon, Sabanakhet, Pakse, and Atapu. The group also maintains a fully equipped distribution center and more than 10 support departments, including HR, IT, accounting and finance, marketing, procurement, packaging, warehousing, audit, and business analysis [1].

## 3. Business Units and Subsidiaries

LDC Group's diverse portfolio includes several key business units:

*   **Jiffy:** A popular convenience store brand offering a wide selection of consumer products at PTT gas stations and neighborhood locations across Laos [1].
*   **Zaap by B Bakery and Meal to Go:** Established in 2016, this unit produces ready-to-eat fresh meals, baked goods, and desserts available at Jiffy and SkyMart stores nationwide. They focus on quality, convenience, and freshness, offering a variety of cuisines including Japanese, European, and local specialties [1].
*   **SkyCool Import-Export Co., Ltd.:** The authorized Daikin dealer in Laos, established in 2017. SkyCool aims to be a central distributor of world-class air conditioning brands like Daikin, Schneider Electric, Philips Lighting, Pyramid Autodoor, AAF, Cytec, Carrier, and Samsung. SkyCool operates a showroom, service center, and training center in Vientiane Capital, supported by over 100 authorized dealers across Laos [1].
*   **CR Service:** Provides equipment and services for minimarts, hotels, and restaurants, including air conditioners, refrigerators, cold rooms, shelving, ice machines, CCTV cameras, microwaves, coffee machines, and maintenance/installation services [1].
*   **Gourmet:** Operates as a restaurant and a supplier of ingredients for general customers, hotels, and restaurants. Their product range includes bakery items, beverage ingredients, wine, dry goods, seasonings, imported meat, and seafood. Gourmet also has an e-commerce platform for product sales and delivery services [1].
*   **Ants E-Commerce:** A newly established e-commerce platform currently preparing for its launch, as mentioned in the video transcription [1]. Further details are not yet publicly available, but the general e-commerce landscape in Laos is seeing developments with new electronic registration systems and platforms being launched [2] [3].
